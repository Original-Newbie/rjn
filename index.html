How I Work

Owners often feel projects move too fast or lack context. My role is to slow the noise down and make decisions clear.

  • Clear sequencing so you know what happens next
  • Realistic schedules based on actual site constraints
  • Straightforward communication without jargon
  • Early risk identification before issues escalate
  • Proactive alignment with AHJs
  • Transparent reporting that keeps owners informed without overwhelm

A Simple, Transparent Process

  1. Understand Goals and Constraints
    • Clarify scope, timing, budget range, and risk tolerance.
  2. Plan and Align the Team
    • Consultants, trades, AHJs, neighbours, and stakeholders brought into a concrete plan.
  3. Manage Execution and Communication
    • Monitor progress, coordinate trades, resolve issues, and keep owners updated.
  4. Deliver with Clarity
    • Turnover, deficiencies, and documentation handled cleanly and predictably.
